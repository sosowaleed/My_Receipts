# About My Receipts

## Vision

In a world of complex, cloud-based financial apps that often require subscriptions and access to your personal banking data, **My Receipts** was created with a different philosophy: **simplicity, privacy, and empowerment**.

Our goal is to provide a powerful yet straightforward tool that helps you understand your financial habits without compromising your privacy. All your data stays with you, on your device, always.

## Core Principles

1.  **Privacy First**: We believe your financial data is yours alone. The app operates completely offline and stores all information in a local SQLite database on your device. There are no servers, no cloud accounts, and no data mining.

2.  **User Empowerment**: Understanding your finances shouldn't require a degree in economics. My Receipts aims to provide clear, actionable insights through its intuitive dashboard. The "what-if" simulation feature is the cornerstone of this principle, transforming the app from a simple expense tracker into a personal financial planning tool. It empowers you to make informed decisions by modeling the future impact of your choices in a safe, sandboxed environment.

3.  **Simplicity and Clarity**: The user interface is designed to be clean, uncluttered, and easy to navigate. Adding a transaction takes seconds. Understanding your monthly cash flow is as simple as looking at a chart. We avoid feature bloat and focus on providing the most impactful tools in the most accessible way.

4.  **Flexibility and Control**: We recognize that one size doesn't fit all. With multi-profile support, customizable categories, and a dual Gregorian/Hijri calendar system, the app adapts to your specific needs. The ability to import and export your data via CSV ensures that you are always in control of your financial history.

## The Journey

This project started as a simple ledger application but has evolved based on the idea that looking backward at your finances is only half the story. True financial literacy comes from looking forward. The addition of intelligent projections and detailed simulations reflects this evolution, providing a tool that not only tracks where you've been but helps you chart a course for where you want to go.

Thank you for using My Receipts. We hope it helps you achieve your financial goals.
